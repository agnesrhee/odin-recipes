Kimbap

Description

This is a classic korean recipe that makes an easy finger food. What's great about kimbap is that it can contain anything your heart desires. "Kim" means seaweed and "bap" means rice, so the most basic ingredients for kimbap are seaweed and rice.

In this recipe, I'll be adding some standard ingredients but feel free to add whatever you'd like. You don't want to add EVERYTHING though, it might overloaded!

Ingredients

  • Roasted seaweed
  • Rice
  • Seasame seeds
  • Yellow radish
  • carrots
  • spam
  • spinach
  • american cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Prepare the vegetables and cutting into same shaped sizes
  3. Saute the vegetables and spam
  4. Once all of the ingredients are prepared, set them aside
  5. Take a piece of seaweed and spread rice all over
  6. line up the veggies and spam at the side of the seaweed
  7. Roll it up and cut into desired slices
