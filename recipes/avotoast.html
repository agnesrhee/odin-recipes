Avocado Toast

Description

This is yet another simple recipe but sometimes the simpliest of recipes are also the tastiest. Who doesn't like bread and avocado? I'm drooling just thinking about it!

You can add whatever you'd like to this recipe or remove whatever you'd like, it's all up to your preferences at this point!

Ingredients

  • Sourdough bread
  • Avocado
  • Coconut oil
  • olive oil
  • Red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Toast the bread with some coconut oil until crispy on the sides.
  2. While it's toasting, slice the avocado in half and remove the pit.
  3. Once the bread is done being toasted, take the avocaodo and smash it with a spoon or fork onto the bread.
  4. If desired, add a little bit of olive oil and a healthy helping of red pepper flakes.
