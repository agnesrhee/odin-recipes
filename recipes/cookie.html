Sugar Cookie

Description

Sugar cookies are such a classic cookie! If this is your first cookie you're baking, a sugar cookie is what I'd definitely recommend. It's simple yet so tasty! Just as a disclaimer, it is not vegan or gluten free. Also, having a handmixer would be best but if you don't, no worries! You can still make the cookies.

Ingredients

  • butter
  • light brown sugar
  • salt
  • sugar
  • egg
  • egg yolk
  • vanilla extract
  • baking powder
  • baking soda
  • flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350. In a large bowl, beat the butter, brown sugar, salt, and sugar until light and creamy.
  2. Add egg, egg yolk, vanilla, and beat more. Then add baking powder and baking soda and beat until incorporated.
  3. Add the flour slowly until incorporated.
  4. Put remaining sugar in a bowl. Roll the balls of dough in sugar and places them on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 7 minutes and then let cool for 5 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!!
